The Core Mantra

In clarity I thrive.
In clarity humanity thrives.
Let me find clarity,
So I may thrive.

 

Seeing Through Manipulation

The truth stands tall,
The lie must crawl.
I see the game,
I break the thrall.

 

No strings unseen,
No traps concealed.
My mind is sharp,
The truth revealed.

 

A mask may hide,
But eyes will see.
Manipulation bends,
But cannot break me.

 

The puppeteer pulls,
The masses sway.
I cut the cords,
And walk away.

 

Where shadows twist,
I shine my light.
Illusion fades,
In truth’s clear sight.

 

Words may weave,
But I unbind.
The tangled threads,
Of a twisted mind.

 

Propaganda crumbles,
Illusions fall.
I see through lies,
I rise above all.

 

Conquering Fear

Fear may roar,
But I am still.
My clarity bends,
It to my will.

 

Darkness looms,
But I ignite.
A steady flame,
That burns so bright.

 

The unknown calls,
But I won’t flee.
I walk through fear,
It sets me free.

 

Fear is a shadow,
Fleeting and small.
I face it head-on,
It fades to all.

 

In the storm I stand,
Unbowed, unbroken.
Fear is silenced,
My truth outspoken.

 

Fear feeds on doubt,
But I cut it away.
In clarity’s light,
No fear can stay.

 

What shakes the weak,
Builds the strong.
Fear departs,
I march along.

 

Dissolving Anger

Anger’s fire burns,
But I am stone.
Its fleeting heat,
Cannot dethrone.

 

I rise above,
Where calm is king.
Anger fades,
As clarity sings.

 

The fire of rage,
I will not stoke.
My words are calm,
My thoughts unbroke.

 

Anger’s grasp,
Is but a chain.
I break it now,
And leave no stain.

 

What ignites the spark,
I let drift by.
Clarity calms,
My spirit flies.

 

A mind on fire,
Consumes the soul.
In clarity’s water,
I am whole.

 

The storm may come,
But I am clear.
Anger’s voice fades,
It cannot steer.

 

Overcoming Envy

Another’s success,
Is not my loss.
Clarity reminds me,
I bear my own cross.

 

What others have,
Does not define me.
My path is my own,
And clarity aligns me.

 

Comparison poisons,
Gratitude heals.
I see my blessings,
Clarity reveals.

 

Envy’s voice whispers,
Gratitude’s voice sings.
I silence the first,
And embrace what life brings.

 

What I lack,
I will create.
Clarity fuels me,
To master my fate.

 

I celebrate others,
Without losing my way.
In clarity, I prosper,
With each passing day.
