Mantras for Authentic Interaction

I see you fully,
I hold no guise.
Truth meets truth,
Through open eyes.

 

What I give,
I also receive.
Heart to heart,
We both believe.

 

The masks are gone,
The air is clear.
What’s real remains,
And draws us near.

 

Each word a bridge,
Each gaze a thread.
We weave the bond,
By truth we're led.

 

Mantras for Being Present

This moment speaks,
It asks my all.
I stay, I hear,
I heed its call.

 

I am here,
I cannot stray.
The world unfolds,
In the now I stay.

 

The past has flown,
The future waits.
This breath, this step,
Opens all gates.

 

The world is vast,
Yet I am still.
What’s here, what’s now,
Will shape my will.

 

Mantras for Engaging with Loved Ones

Your voice is heard,
Your soul is felt.
Through open hearts,
The walls will melt.

 

We are the same,
Yet each unique.
In shared exchange,
The truth we seek.

 

I offer myself,
Without disguise.
We meet as one,
Where truth resides.

 

The bond we build,
Grows root and stem.
In presence shared,
We bloom again.

 

Mantras for Connecting with Yourself

I meet myself,
With open hands.
No need to flee,
Where truth commands.

 

The storm within,
Begins to fade.
In the calm I find,
What I am made.

 

I see my shadow,
I hold its face.
What I embrace,
Begins to grace.

 

I walk the path,
Through joy and strife.
I meet myself,
And come to life.

 

Mantras for Engaging with Society

I see the web,
Each thread connects.
We move as one,
What one affects.

 

A single act,
Becomes a wave.
Together we rise,
Together we save.

 

What builds the world,
Is shared by all.
Each hand, each heart,
Answers the call.

 

The many gather,
To form the whole.
Each voice, each soul,
Plays its role.
